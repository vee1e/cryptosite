About Us

cryptonite@mit ~ /about
cryptonite@mit:~$ whoami

Cryptonite is the official cybersecurity and ethical hacking student project at Manipal Institute of Technology (MAHE). We are dedicated to advancing the field of cybersecurity through research, education, and practical application.

cryptonite@mit:~$ cat mission.txt

To explore cutting-edge security methodologies, analyze cryptographic systems, and develop expertise in both hardware and software security through hands-on experience and research initiatives.

cryptonite@mit:~$ ls activities/
  • > Capture-The-Flag (CTF) competitions and training
  • > Research in cybersecurity and cryptography
  • > Hardware and software security analysis
  • > Educational workshops and knowledge sharing
cryptonite@mit:~$ cat initiatives.md
We organize flagship events like Cryptober and niteCTF, bringing together cybersecurity enthusiasts from around the world for collaborative learning and innovation.
